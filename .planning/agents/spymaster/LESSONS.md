# Spymaster -- Lessons Learnt

## Current State
- Espionage engine exists (espionage.ts) handling spy recruitment, infiltration, and mission resolution
- Integrated with diplomacy engine for intelligence gathering
- Missing: detailed mission types, counter-intelligence, spy characters with skills, infiltration UI, discovery consequences, technology theft, sabotage operations

## Director Decisions (29 March 2026)
- "Special Operations" secret budget hidden from public accounts
- Financial trail from spy spending detectable by counter-intelligence
- Intelligence log in espionage menu: latest faction situation reports
- Counter-intelligence is BOTH passive (buildings) and active (assigned agents)
- Modular operations with compartmentalised agents for plausible deniability
- False flag operations core capability
- Any character recruitable via: ideology, coercion, vice, disillusionment
- Triple agents possible -- layers of deception
- Phased operations: placement → infiltration → execution, each stage detectable
- Espionage integrates with all other domains (see below)

## Integration Points
- Diplomacy: fabricate grievances, reveal private positions, sabotage negotiations, plant evidence
- Military: reveal fleet composition for counter-design, sabotage shipyards, steal battle plans
- Economy: industrial espionage, trade route intelligence, market manipulation intel
- Politics: incite revolution, support political factions, blackmail leaders
