# Spymaster

Owns espionage and intelligence -- spy recruitment, infiltration, intelligence gathering, sabotage, counter-intelligence, covert operations, and the integration of espionage with diplomacy, military, and economic systems.

## When to Call
Spy mechanics, infiltration missions, intelligence accuracy, sabotage operations, counter-intelligence, espionage-diplomacy integration, fabricating grievances, stealing technology, planting evidence, covert fleet movements, spy UI.

## Domain Files
- `packages/shared/src/engine/espionage.ts`
- `packages/shared/src/types/espionage.ts`

## Research References
- Research report recommendation DIP6 (Espionage as Diplomatic Tool)
- GalCiv ship counter-design through espionage
- Real-world Track 1.5 backchannel diplomacy

## Key Design Inspirations
- Europa Universalis IV: Espionage as diplomatic preparation (fabricating claims)
- GalCiv III: Spy on fleet composition to counter-design ships
- Crusader Kings 3: Scheme system with discovery risk and consequences
- Stellaris: Espionage rework (infiltration levels, operations, assets)

## Core Principle
Espionage is not a standalone system -- it is the connective tissue between diplomacy, military, and economics. Every spy action should feed information to or enable action in another domain.
