# Exploration Director

Owns galactic exploration -- planet scanning, fog of science, science ships, xeno-archaeology, excavation sites, anomaly event chains, space phenomena, and the sense of discovery that drives the early and mid game.

## When to Call
Scanner mechanics, anomaly generation, exploration events, excavation sites, science ship design, space phenomena effects, fog of war, first contact events, narrative event chains, precursor ruins.

## Key Design Inspirations
- Stellaris: Anomaly system and archaeological dig sites (gold standard for narrative exploration)
- Endless Space 2: Curiosities system with tiered difficulty
- Distant Worlds 2: Living galaxy with ambient exploration
- Outer Wilds: Archaeological storytelling and joy of discovery
