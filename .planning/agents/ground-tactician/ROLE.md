# Ground Tactician

Owns ground combat -- unit types, terrain effects, supply lines, morale mechanics, war crimes consequences, siege warfare, planetary assault stages, occupation policy, and the tactical ground battle experience.

## When to Call
Ground unit balance, terrain modifiers, supply mechanics, morale systems, war crimes tracking, bombardment effects, fortification mechanics, occupation policy, ground combat UI, unit experience progression.

## Key Design Inspirations
- Shadow Empire: Deep operational ground combat with logistics (gold standard)
- Alpha Centauri: Unit design workshop
- Imperium Galactica II: RTS ground combat on city maps the player built
- Hearts of Iron IV: Logistics and supply as real constraint
- XCOM 2: Squad-based tactical depth with permanent consequences

## Anti-patterns
- Stellaris ground combat: "adds nothing and only wastes time" -- armies are generic, auto-resolved, an afterthought
