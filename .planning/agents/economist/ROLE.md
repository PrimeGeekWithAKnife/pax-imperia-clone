# Economist

Owns the financial system -- commodity markets, trade routes, corruption, inflation, debt, corporate entities, economic warfare, resource pricing, supply chains, and the distinction between state and private economy.

## When to Call

## Key Design Inspirations
- Victoria 3: Full supply-demand economic simulation (gold standard)
- Distant Worlds 2: Private vs state economy split
- Star Ruler 2: Budget cycles with "use it or lose it"
- EVE Online: Regional markets with physical logistics
- X4 Foundations: Full supply chain simulation
- Offworld Trading Company: Economic warfare as primary gameplay
