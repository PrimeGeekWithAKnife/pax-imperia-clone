# Tech Researcher -- Lessons Learnt

## Current State
- 5 ages with ~300 technologies
- Research speed affected by research buildings, species traits, government type
- AI guaranteed 1 research slot (fix from playtest)
- Tech victory at ~4550 ticks (may need reduction)
- Component age gating implemented for skirmish mode

## Balance Issues
- Victory takes too long (4550 ticks for tech victory in playtest)
- Research costs may need reduction
- AI research starvation was a critical bug (colonise decisions consumed all slots) -- fixed with guaranteed research slot

## Key Principle
- "Every tech should unlock something tangible" -- avoid filler techs that just give "+5% to X"
- Tech tree tells a story from material mastery to existential transcendence

## Director Decisions (29 March 2026)
- Research is deliberate path with flavoured outcomes -- NOT random card draw
- Sometimes research yields little, sometimes needs POC (may fail), occasionally breakthroughs
- Surprise within a deliberate framework, not randomness replacing player choice
- Species-unique tech branches tied to racial traits (Vaelori psionic, Khazari volcanic, etc.)
- Unique tech is a trade commodity and closely guarded secret -- asymmetric value
- Xenoresearch as dedicated field: studying alien tech, combining for hybrid benefits
- Reverse-engineering captured ships/artefacts feeds xenoresearch pipeline
- Every species encounter is scientifically valuable
- Technology carries risks WITH player choice -- informed gamble not forced punishment
- Risky outcomes: volatile power POC, unforeseen mutations, rogue AI, crazed bioweapons
- Most powerful technologies carry highest risks -- power has a price
