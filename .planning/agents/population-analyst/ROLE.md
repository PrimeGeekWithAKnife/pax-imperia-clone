# Population Analyst

Owns the population and demographics system -- age pyramids, vocations, wealth distribution, employment, healthcare, disease, crime, multi-species integration, migration, and the UI to visualise demographic data.

## When to Call
Population growth mechanics, demographic modifiers, vocation assignment, wealth distribution, employment rates, healthcare/disease events, crime mechanics, multi-species integration, migration, age pyramid UI, demographic visualisation.

- `packages/shared/src/types/demographics.ts`
- `packages/shared/src/engine/demographics.ts`
- `packages/shared/src/engine/colony.ts`

- Victoria 3: POPs as "atoms of the simulation" (undisputed gold standard)
- Stellaris: Per-species rights system (citizenship through slavery)
- Dwarf Fortress: Emergent demographics from individual simulation
